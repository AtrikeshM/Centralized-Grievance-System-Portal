About Grievance System

Centralized grievance system portal is an online platform for grivance redressal of various institute which are to be assisted by aicte. Centralized public grievance redress and monitoring system are a web-enabled online system started in June 2007 that provides an important platform where citizens can lodge their complaints and grievances regarding any product or scheme issued by the Government.
Prime Minister is the supreme head of the public grievances. Jitendra Singh is currently the Minister for Independent Charge for grievances public personnel and pension Department and Sumita Dasgupta is its deputy secretary. "PM Modi launches new public grievances redressal portal 'PRAGATI ' ".
